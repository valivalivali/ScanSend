## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is ScanSend really free?**  
A: Yes! You can scan up to 3 documents for free. For unlimited scanning, upgrade to Pro for $4.99/month.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**  
A: No account needed! Start scanning immediately after download.

**Q: Where are my documents stored?**  
A: All documents are stored locally on your iPhone. We never upload your data to any servers.

**Q: Can I use ScanSend offline?**  
A: Yes! ScanSend works 100% offline. No internet required.

### Scanning Questions

**Q: How do I scan a multi-page document?**  
A: After scanning the first page, tap "Add Page" to continue scanning additional pages. All pages will be combined into one PDF.

**Q: Why is my scan blurry?**  
A: Make sure to:
- Hold your phone steady
- Have good lighting
- Let the camera auto-focus
- Keep the document flat

**Q: Can I import existing photos?**  
A: Yes! Tap the **+** button and select "Import from Photos" to convert existing images to PDF.

**Q: What file types can I import?**  
A: ScanSend supports PDF, PNG, JPEG, and HEIC files.

### Sharing & Export

**Q: What format are documents exported in?**

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Camera Issues

**Problem: Camera won't open**  
Solution:
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Camera
2. Make sure ScanSend is enabled
3. If not listed, delete and reinstall the app

**Problem: "Camera access denied" message**  
Solution:
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Camera
2. Toggle ScanSend permission off and on
3. Restart the app

### App Crashes

**Problem: App crashes on launch**  
Solution:
1. Force quit the app (swipe up and remove)
2. Restart your iPhone
3. Update to the latest iOS version
4. Delete and reinstall ScanSend

**Problem: App crashes when scanning**  
Solution:
1. Free up storage space on your device
2. Close other apps running in background
3. Try scanning with fewer pages

### Storage Issues

**Problem: "Storage full" message**  
Solution:
1. Delete unused documents in ScanSend
2. Free up iPhone storage in Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí iPhone Storage
3. Consider upgrading to Pro for unlimited storage

### Subscription Issues

**Problem: Pro features not working after purchase**  
Solution:
1. Tap the menu ‚Üí Restore Purchases
2. Make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
3. Wait 5 minutes for the purchase to process
4. Restart the app
